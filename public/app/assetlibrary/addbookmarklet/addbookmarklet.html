Add assets to the Asset Library from other websites

1. Turn on your browser's bookmarks bar

Make sure your Bookmarks bar is turned on. If it isn't, go to the browser menu > view > Toolbars > Bookmarks Toolbar and turn it on there.

2. Drag the bookmarklet to the bookmarks bar

Add the Asset Library bookmarklet displayed on the right to your browser's bookmarks bar by simply dragging and dropping it there.
Add inspiration

3. Save assets with one click

Save an interesting website, image or video by clicking the bookmarklet in your browser bookmarks bar.
